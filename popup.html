How to use:

  1. Go to live match in chess.com
  2. Wait for match with an opponent
  3. Fill in your pieces color (w for white, b for black)
  4. Once you found a match, press Troll Now! and make any first move
  5. An alert box will show up with chess moves
  6. After your game is finished, refresh the page before starting a new one, repeat the steps
